We would love to see our family and friends get dressed up! The dress code is Semi-Formal. We suggest Suit/Barong for men and Floor-length/Cocktail dresses for women.
We kindly ask that you avoid wearing shades of ivory. We’d love for you to complement our theme colors, which include burgundy and white.
Due to limited seating at our venue, we can only accommodate those specifically listed on your invitation.
We certainly love your kids, but due to necessity rather than choice, we regretfully cannot accommodate them at the venue. The only kids attending are those who are part of the entourage and those requested. We hope you understand and enjoy the day!
If you are late to the ceremony, please do not walk down the aisle and try to find a seat. While we want all of our friends and family there, we would appreciate if you would ask assistance from our coordinator or wait to join us until the reception. We thank you for understanding.
We would love for you to help us capture the joy of our day! However, we have a professional photography and videography team working hard to document every moment. We kindly ask that you:

• Be Mindful of the Pros: Please ensure your cameras or phones do not obstruct the view or equipment of our official media team.

• Clear the Path: To keep the ceremony flowing beautifully, please refrain from stepping into the aisle or blocking the path of the bride and the entourage.

We can’t wait to see the memories you capture from your seat!
